Our Leadership

Pastor Susan Kibii

Susan Kibii is an accomplished communication consultant, counsellor, leadership expert, author, lecturer, and minister of the Gospel, with extensive experience in leadership development and personal transformation.

She has dedicated her career to empowering individuals and organizations by creating platforms for knowledge sharing, training, and skills development across corporate, academic, church, and community settings.

Over the years, Susan has authored twelve books, including works focused on women's empowerment, such as The Woman You Are Meant to Be and Woman in Pieces: Restoring the Dignity of Womanhood.
